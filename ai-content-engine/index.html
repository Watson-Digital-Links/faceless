Frequently Asked Questions.

Do I need to show my face?

No. Tier 1 is faceless. Tier 2 uses a short, one-time tripod recording to train your personal avatar and capture a matching voice; after setup, you can produce new talking-head content without filming each Reel.

Do I have to write my own scripts?

No. The system uses AI (Allen AI, ChatGPT, Claude) to write scripts for you. You can approve them as-is, tweak them, or write your own from scratch. Most users never write a single script — they just approve and go.

What is the Tier 2 cinematic stack?

HeyGen generates the voice track. Hedra provides access to Seedance 2.0 for talking-head lip sync and Kling O3 Pro for cinematic motion B-roll. The two clips render in parallel, pass a still-frame quality check, and are assembled into the final Reel.

How much does ongoing operation cost?

Tier 1 can run on a lean stack using voice generation, stock B-roll, and local assembly. Tier 2's validated configuration uses Hedra Pro at $75/month for 14,400 credits, plus any separate voice or scripting subscriptions you choose. Model pricing changes, so verify current plan limits before purchasing.

How long until I see results?

There is no fixed timeline. The first month builds a performance baseline; after that, improve the topics, hooks, formats, and CTAs that earn the strongest response. Reach and leads depend on the market and execution.

Do I need technical skills?

If you can follow step-by-step instructions, you can build this. The modules include exact commands, scripts, and screenshots. No coding required.

Can I use this for multiple businesses?

The system is designed for one Instagram account. If you manage multiple, each needs its own pipeline — but the training applies to all of them.

Why not just use VEED, CapCut, or Canva?

Those are useful editing tools. This course adds the production architecture around them: idea research, script templates, faceless volume production, optional personal-avatar lip sync, cinematic B-roll, QA, batching, and publishing. The value is the repeatable workflow, not a single editing app.
